# Third-party assets bundled in `data/`

## Fonts

- `fonts/RobotoMono-Regular.fnt`
- `fonts/RobotoMono-Bold.fnt`

  Bitmap-font renderings of **Roboto Mono** in the PiBoSo plugin `.fnt` font
  format. Roboto Mono is © Google and licensed under the
  **Apache License, Version 2.0** (https://www.apache.org/licenses/LICENSE-2.0).
  Used here as the overlay font for the roadbook plugin.

## Icons (`icons/rb_*.tga`)

Rally sign sprites (white, 64x64 TGA), built by
`tools/iconpack/make_tulip.mjs` + `make_tulip_tga.py`:

- **Most signs** — terrain, track, danger, abbreviations and scenery (bump,
  dip, ditch, compression, hole, ruts, rough, ford/water, narrow, cliff,
  collapse, fence, gate, bridge, pole, antenna, pipeline, rocks, tree,
  village, church, animals, danger-1/2/3, off-track-forbidden, fuel, and the
  kpL/kpR/kpS/L/R/onL/onR abbreviations) — are rasterized from the **authentic
  FIA/Dakar roadbook glyph set** of the open-source **tulip** roadbook editor
  by *drid* (https://gitlab.com/drid/tulip), licensed
  **GNU General Public License v2.0 or later**. These glyph TGAs (and any
  build that bundles them) therefore fall under the GPL v2+.
- **info, north, stop** — rendered from the free **Rally Symbols** font
  (dafont.com), as the tulip set has no direct equivalent.
- `icons/rb_arrow.tga` — the tulip exit arrowhead, rasterized from the
  `TriangleStart` route-arrow marker of the same **tulip** project (GPL v2+).

## Device bodies (`icons/rb_device0..22.tga`)

The 23 rally-computer device skins (carbon body, LCD bezels, screen window,
buttons) are **original artwork** generated procedurally by
`tools/iconpack/make_device.py` (no third-party assets); free to use.

## Audio (`audio/rb_*.wav`)

The warning tones (chime / danger / off-route) are **original** synthesized
sine tones generated by `tools/iconpack/make_audio.py`; free to use.
