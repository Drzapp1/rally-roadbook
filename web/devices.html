The in-game roadbook can be shown on any of 23 rally-computer skins. Each body's finish is rendered from a real Creative-Commons material photo (carbon-fibre weave, brushed aluminium, knurled metal) with lit bevels and a recessed screen. The roadbook-holder models (RaidNav, Tripmaster) carry knurled wind-knobs. Pick one in-game from F4 → Rally computer. (Preview shows a sample roadbook on the screen.)
